    Most Recommended Languages

    Overwhelmingly we found that the most recommended language to start with was Python. To even display this word chart, we had to dramatically scale the responses. Some languages offered as options in the dataset received so few recommendations that we removed them all together from the base chart, such as TypeScript.

    The popularity of Python is understandable given the wide range of uses it has and variety of libraries to increase its functionality even further, many of which are represented below in the bubble chart demonstration of what is used most often by working professionals.

    Filtering by job types appears to only change which language takes the runner up prize, notably shifting towards either SQL or R depending on the filter. This is understandable given the different roles and responsibilities of the differing jobs, which may favor certain activities over others, such as the choice between visualizations or analysis where R and MATLAB may win out and database management where SQL would have the edge.

    Overall though we can confidently say that if you are getting your feet wet in the data professions, almost everyone agrees that Python is where you should start.

    Industry Favorites

    Languages Text Editors/Environments Visualization Libraries Databases Machine Learning Methods Learning Platforms

    From our dataset we had available the ratings individuals gave on what tools and languages they used on a regular basis. We put this together to see if we could visualize trends in which ones were the most popular overall or by job type and made some interesting discoveries.

    Our favorite find was in the category of learning platform, where Coursera was rated as the most popular overall, something that we will all be keeping in mind when we want to go pick up new topics. It also appears that Data and Business Analysts are spending the most time on the various learning platforms, as all seem to have much larger shares when filtering only for those jobs.
