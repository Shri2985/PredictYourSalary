    • Does background matter?

    As the world shifts towards becoming more technologically driven many individuals have changed careers to join this wave. We wanted to see how much a computer science background played a role in determining salary across the different jobs in our dataset. We were happy to see, as shown above, that there were no noticeable trends in how well people with different backgrounds were represented across the industry. Our assumption is that demonstration of technical skills plays far more of a role than what an individual majored in, which is an uplifting finding for anyone considering shifting into this branch of the STEM market.
