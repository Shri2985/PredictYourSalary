    • Technology and Age

    The tech industry has a long reputation for being biased towards youth. And while this may play out in some areas, our analysis of salaries by age found a natural progression of people earning more money as they got older. This is presumably proportional to the benefit of gaining experience over time and moving up in company structures that is reflected in how well paid individuals get as they age.
